Secure vs Unhealthy Comparison

Same feeling. Different nervous-system strategy.

Secure

Names needs directly, tolerates space, repairs rupture, and does not confuse calm with disinterest.

Anxious

Seeks reassurance quickly, reads silence as threat, over-functions emotionally, and escalates for closeness.

Avoidant

Protects autonomy, minimizes needs, delays responses, and may experience intimacy as pressure.
